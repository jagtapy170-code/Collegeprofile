The college offers UG engineering degree courses in Civil Engineering, Computer Engineering, and Electrical Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics & Telecommunication, Artificial Intelligence & Data Science, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Robotics & Automation, Electronics & Computer Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. The college has the experienced and highly qualified staff, specious classrooms and well equipped laboratories.
